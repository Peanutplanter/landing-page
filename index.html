FAQ

How do I choose the right tutor for me?
You can browse tutor profiles on our platform, which detail their qualifications, teaching style, and areas of expertise. We also offer a free initial consultation to help you find the tutor that best matches your learning preferences and goals.
Can I change my tutor if I'm not satisfied?
Absolutely. Your satisfaction and progress are our top priorities. If you feel your current tutor isn't the right fit, you can easily switch to another tutor.
What happens if I miss a scheduled lesson?
We understand that unexpected events can occur. If you miss a lesson, please contact us 24 hours in advance to reschedule. We offer a flexible rescheduling policy to accommodate your needs.
Do you provide materials, or should I get my own?
Our tutors provide all the necessary materials for the lessons. However, they may recommend additional resources that you can choose to use to supplement your learning.
What if I have specific goals or need to focus on certain areas?
Our lessons are fully personalized. During your initial consultation and ongoing sessions, your tutor will tailor the curriculum to focus on your specific goals, whether it's business English, academic writing, or conversational fluency.
